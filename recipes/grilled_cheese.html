Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

This is a grilled cheese. The sandwich of our childhoods.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of Sourdough Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Pepperjack Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Place all the cheese on a slice of bread
  2. Place the second slice of bread on top of all the cheese
  3. Put sandwich in pan and grill it
